# Data included in the public manuscript replication package

This folder contains processed analytical inputs distributed with the public package.

## Figure-ready inputs in `data/`

- `upf_exposure_by_age_and_sex.csv`
  Age- and sex-specific UPF exposure estimates used in Figure 1.

- `upf_attributable_deaths_by_age_and_sex.csv`
  Age- and sex-specific attributable deaths and population attributable fractions used in Figure 1.

- `environmental_baseline_shares.csv`
  Baseline contribution of UPFs to greenhouse gas emissions, land use, water use, and freshwater eutrophication used in Figure 2.

- `scenario_health_economic_results.csv`
  Scenario-level health and economic results used in the manuscript scenario panels.

- `scenario_environment_results.csv`
  Scenario-level environmental results used in Figures 2 and 4.

- `replacement_basket_composition.csv`
  Food-group composition of the replacement baskets used in Figure 3.

## Calculation-ready inputs in `data/analysis/`

The `analysis/` subfolder contains the processed inputs used by the ordered calculation pipeline in `scripts/calculations/`.

See [analysis/README.md](analysis/README.md) for the file-level dictionary.
